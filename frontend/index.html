Frequently Asked Questions

What parameters does WebShield AI evaluate?
WebShield AI performs checks on five primary security markers: DNSSEC record configs, SSL/TLS certificate chains and cipher version support, HTTP Security Headers (HSTS, CSP, X-Frame-Options), system port listings, and exposed directories/backups.
Will this scan negatively impact my server performance?
No. The checks are purely non-intrusive metadata reviews of public headers, certificate handshake endpoints, and DNS registry zones. We do not conduct high-traffic DDoS tests or brute-force logins.
How is the Security Score calculated?
The rating starts at 100. Deductions are dynamically applied based on vulnerability severity weights: Critical items (expired certificates, open DB ports) deduct 20-25 points, high items (missing CSP) deduct 15 points, and low items (server metadata leaks) deduct 3-5 points.
